# Challenge 04: Stash and Seek (Medium)

## Objective
Use stash to pause incomplete work and switch context.

## Files
- `notes.md`
- `hotfix.md`

## Task
1. In `notes.md`, add two unfinished bullet points under TODO.
2. Do NOT commit yet.
3. Stash your work with message:
   - `challenge-04 unfinished notes`
4. Switch to `main` and update `hotfix.md` by adding:
   - `Hotfix applied by <your-name>`
5. Commit:
   - `challenge-04: applied urgent hotfix`
6. Re-apply your stash and verify `notes.md` changes are back.
7. Commit restored note changes:
   - `challenge-04: restored and completed notes`

## Success Criteria
- At least one stash entry created and applied.
- Two commits exist for this challenge.
